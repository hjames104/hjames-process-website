Design Challenge

The first step in the process was defining the Design Challenge by utilizing the IDEO Human Centered Design Toolkit.

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

To empower those suffering the effects of trauma, neglect and hardships through the use of rescued horses in Equine Assisted Therapies.

The Design Question

How does the Mending Strides website help empower individuals through the use of Equine Assisted Therapies?

The Ultimate Impact

To increase donations and volunteers in order to reach the most people possible.

Possible Solutions

High quality images, clear hierarchy, emphasis on donations, increased ease of usability, organization, and readable typefaces

Context and Constraints

Mending Strides is a relatively new nonprofit, so they do not have an excess of information or an established customer base to work with.They offer very specialized services that require explanation. Mending Strides is in Charlotte, and I am not there very often to interact with them directly.

Design Question Revised

How can Mending Strides website best attract and serve both donors/volunteers and potential customers in order to most effectively empower individuals through the use of Equine Assisted Therapies?
